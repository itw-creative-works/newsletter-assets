The latest wave of AI products is moving from chat to concrete work: finding the right data, completing admin tasks, and building live web apps from corporate systems. The pattern is clear, the best agents are not general-purpose magic, but focused tools with enough context to do one job reliably. For SMBs, that points to a practical playbook: start with narrow workflows where the inputs are structured, the outcome is measurable, and the risk of a wrong answer is low.
